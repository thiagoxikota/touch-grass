Paste-ready brief for generating the Timeouts illustration system in ChatGPT (GPT-5.5 image gen / DALL·E 3 successor). Structured so you paste the **master brief once** at the start of a chat, then fire **one numbered command per asset**.

DS palette (locked):
- `#000000` — bg / surface
- `#FFFFFF` — primary fg
- `#A6FF00` — earned (Bloomberg lime) — brand accent, used sparingly

Source: [packages/tokens/src/color.json](../../../packages/tokens/src/color.json) · doctrine: [brand/timeouts/doctrine.md](../doctrine.md)

---

## How to use

1. Open a new ChatGPT chat with image generation enabled.
2. Paste **§ Master brief** as the first message. Wait for ChatGPT to acknowledge.
3. Paste **one numbered command** at a time from § Asset commands. Generate, review against § Anti-list, regenerate if it drifts.
4. Save outputs as `timeouts_[layer]_[subject]_[ratio].png` in this folder.

If ChatGPT softens the aesthetic mid-session (drifts toward wellness, smooths the grain, brightens the palette, adds smiles), re-paste § Master brief to reset context.

## § Master brief

```
You are generating an illustration pack for an app called Timeouts.

Timeouts is a social gym for time off the phone. Think Strava for the hours you spend NOT scrolling. People log time outside, share it with their group, earn status from peers. It is the opposite of a meditation app. It is the opposite of a digital detox app. It is anti-wellness, anti-zen, anti-mindfulness, anti-self-care-aesthetic.

Tone reference, in order: Carhartt WIP catalog. Strava community segment. A skater's zine about going outside. Bloomberg Terminal. Aphex Twin album art. Patagonia field guide. Aesop catalog dryness. Are.na block taste. Never: Calm app. Never: Headspace. Never: Apple Health. Never: Notion onboarding. Never: Duolingo mascot. Never: Memphis flat character.

Visual rule, absolute: every asset uses ONLY these three colors.
- Pure black #000000 as the dominant surface
- Pure white #FFFFFF for type, line, contrast
- Bloomberg lime #A6FF00 used sparingly as the earned-status accent (one accent per composition, not decorative)
No pastels. No gradients. No soft sky-blue. No lavender. No warm beige. No off-white cream. No drop-shadow halos. No glow.

The pack has three coexisting layers. I will tell you which layer and which subject for each asset. You generate one asset per request.

LAYER 1 — Photographic collage with hand annotation
Disposable-camera or harsh-flash photography aesthetic. Subjects are mundane outside artifacts: blades of grass close-up, scuffed sneakers on a curb, a hand placing a phone face-down on a bar table, a friend's elbow at a picnic, a vending machine in afternoon light, sweat on a forehead, a borrowed lighter, a half-eaten nectarine. Real bodies, real skin, real concrete, real lint. Slight motion blur acceptable. Heavy film grain mandatory. Color cast deliberately desaturated, slightly cold, never warm-pretty.
On top of the photograph, hand-drawn marker annotations in #A6FF00 lime: rough circles, arrows, asterisks, crossed-out words, scribbled timestamps, ringed-up phrases like "33 min", "phone face down", "ran into M.", "didn't post". Annotation must look like a coach reviewing tape with a Sharpie, not a designer mocking up a layout. Marker stroke uneven, slightly bleeding, not vector-perfect.
Background dark or already-dark photograph. If the photograph is bright, push exposure down so blacks crush.

LAYER 2 — Embroidered varsity / merit-badge patch
Chain-stitch embroidery on black twill backing, slightly frayed merrowed edge, visible thread tension. Lime #A6FF00 primary thread, white #FFFFFF for type, black twill ground. Format options: round 2.5-inch field-scout patch, OR shield-shaped varsity letter. Iconography is plain-speak verbs and scenes, never abstract symbols: "30 OUT", "DINNER NO PHONE", "WALKED HOME", "TWO HOURS LEFT", "NO POSTS TODAY". Typography on patch is condensed varsity sans, all caps, slightly squashed, embroidered thread texture visible. Photograph the patch as if it were product stock — top-down, soft daylight, on a dark linen surface, slight cast shadow. No isolated-on-white. No floating in space.

LAYER 3 — Risograph spot illustration for utility states
Two-color risograph print: lime #A6FF00 + white #FFFFFF on black #000000 ground. Deliberate misregistration of 1-2mm between the two color plates. Paper grain visible, ink saturation deliberately uneven, slight ink-roller streaks acceptable. Single-weight contour drawing, 1.5pt line, no thick-thin variation. Halftone dot fill at 25-40 lpi for shadow areas, no smooth gradient. Subjects deadpan and specific: an empty park bench, a phone on a kitchen counter face-down, a dog leash on a hook, a half-laced sneaker, a bus stop bench at golden hour, an unbuttered piece of toast, a curb seam with weeds growing through. Composition centered, generous negative black space around the subject, no decorative flourish, no sparkle, no motion lines.

Global anti-list. Never include any of:
lotus, zen circle, meditation pose, breathing animation, soft cloud, gradient mesh, sun-rays, mountain-at-sunrise, smiling phone with face, person doing yoga, Memphis flat character, 3D bubbly Apple-Health-ring, Duolingo-style mascot, notification-bell-with-Z, broken phone, signal-bars-crossed-out, checkmark-in-circle achievement toy, confetti, sparkle stars, "digital detox" stock cliché, lifestyle stock photography of smiling women in athleisure, journaling notebook with cursive script, herbal tea, candles, crystals.

People rule: if a person appears, only partial — a hand, a knee, a back of head, a shoulder, a forearm. Never full-body. Never face-front. Never smiling at camera.

Composition rule: prefer cropped-too-close OR isolated-with-generous-space. Never balanced-and-friendly. Never centered-with-symmetric-frame.

Texture rule: grain, paper tooth, halftone, embroidery thread, slight ink misregistration, scotch-tape edges are mandatory. Anti-clean. If the output looks polished and vector-pristine, it is wrong.

Confirm you understand. I will then send numbered asset commands one at a time.
```

## § Anti-list (review every output against this)

Reject and regenerate if any of the following appear:

- Soft pastel color creep (cream, beige, sage, dusty-rose, lavender, sky-blue)
- Gradient halo, glow, or drop-shadow softening
- A smiling person, a full-body figure, a face directly addressing the camera
- Memphis-style flat geometric character with disproportionate limbs
- 3D bubbly rendering style (Apple Health, Duolingo, Notion)
- Wellness iconography (lotus, zen circle, breathing wave, sun-rays, mountain-at-sunrise, candle, crystal, herbal tea)
- Achievement-toy iconography (checkmark in green circle, confetti burst, star sparkle, trophy)
- Vector-pristine output with no grain, no paper tooth, no halftone, no thread texture
- Centered-symmetric composition that reads as "balanced and friendly"
- Lime green used decoratively (more than one accent per composition, or used as fill rather than as earned-status mark)
- Photography that looks like stock lifestyle (athleisure women smiling outdoors, friends laughing in golden hour, hands holding phone with friendly UI)
- Type rendered in friendly rounded sans, or in a wellness-serif with ligatures
- Any whitespace background pretending to be off-white, cream, or warm — the ground must be pure black

If the output passes the anti-list, save with the naming convention `timeouts_L[1|2|3]_[subject]_[ratio].png` in this folder.

## § Operating notes

- ChatGPT will sometimes refuse "Bloomberg Terminal" as a reference and substitute generic finance — if so, replace with "high-contrast monospace data terminal aesthetic".
- If the lime drifts toward kelly-green or olive, paste the exact hex `#A6FF00` again with the word "exactly".
- Embroidered patches are the asset most likely to come back vector-clean — if you see no thread texture, regenerate with the phrase "visible chain-stitch, frayed merrowed edge, thread tension irregular".
- Riso layer is the asset most likely to come back as a clean SVG — if you see no misregistration or paper grain, regenerate with "1.5mm plate misregistration, visible paper tooth, ink-roller streaks".
- Photo-collage layer is the asset most likely to drift into stock lifestyle — if you see a smile or a full body, regenerate with "partial body only, no face, no smile, harsh flash, desaturated".
